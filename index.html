About Dr. Harrington

Dr. James Harrington is a highly respected Consultant Physician based in Harley Street, London. He trained at University College London and completed his specialist training at Guy’s & St Thomas’ and The Royal Free Hospital.


He specializes in complex medical conditions, chronic disease management, preventive medicine, and provides clear, compassionate care to every patient.
